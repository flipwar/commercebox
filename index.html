Section 164

in this chapter unless the context otherwise requires,-

  1. "applellate tribunal" means the appellate tribunal constitue under the section 252 of the Income tax Act;

"Assessing office" means the Income tax Officer or Assistant Commissioner of Income Tax or Income tax or Additional Commissioner of Income tax who is authorised by the Board to exercise of perform all or any of the powers and function conferred on, or assigner to, an Assessing office under this chapter;
